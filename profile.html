Company Profile

Gentry Roofing is one of Kansas City’s premier roofing companies has been operating in the Greater Kansas City area since 1971. Tim Gentry, who began working for his father’s Lake Tahoe, California based roofing company when he was just 13 years old, founded Gentry Roofing in Kansas City. He operated it based on the principle that good work done for a fair price will make a company successful. John Howard, Tim’s nephew, started working for Gentry Roofing in 1984 and became President of the company in 1999. John still follows the same principles his family founded the business upon. He believes in high standards and outstanding customer service whether you need a new roof or a re-roof. Thousands of satisfied customers later, Gentry Roofing is still in business and stronger than ever.

The main value of a licensed roofing company is to do high-quality work and still be in business in order to be there for its customers for the life of their roofs. Gentry Roofing has demonstrated that value for 35 years. A well respected Family business based on quality, customer service and that personal touch…Gentry Roofing plans to continue these traditions of excellence for generations to come.
