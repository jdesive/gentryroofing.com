Customer Testimonial from Jenny in Fairway, KS

December 7, 2010

When I bought my home it had a 20 year old asphalt roof on it. It also had 3 layers of roof, so because of city ordinances, I had to remove all three layers (three layers was the limit) and start from scratch.

Gentry Roofing was recommended to me by a family friend – so I decided to go with them. I also knew that I wanted a DaVinci Slate Gray roof in the staggered pattern – and I knew that Gentry Roofing and John Howard were pros at installing DaVinci.

My roof is a 50-year roof – I am only 5 years into the 50 years, but so far so good! I might not have to use Gentry Roofing again – but should I move and need another new roof, I will certainly give them a call. I would recommend them to anyone.
